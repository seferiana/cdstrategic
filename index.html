About Us

Michael Bio here

Seferiana Day has over 10 years of experience in campaigns, outreach, and organizing. She most recently served as a Special Advisor to Mayor Tim Burgess, and for nearly three years as his Legislative Assistant on the Seattle City Council. She was instrumental in the passage of legislation relating to Short-term Rentals, the 2016 Seattle Housing Levy, and the Sweetened Beverage Tax, and wrote legislation establishing the Seattle Renters' Commission. She has worked on campaigns for Seattle City Council, including Hisam Goueli's 2017 race and Tim Burgess's 2015 re-election campaign. She managed Congresswoman Pramila Jayapal's 2014 campaign for State Senate, and organized for the $15 minimum wage in Sea-Tac.


Services We Offer

Political Campaigns


We offer a full suite of services to run a succesful campaign:

  • Field and Outreach Plans
  • Graphic and Web Design
  • Policy Guidance
  • Strategic Communications and Messaging

Policy Advocacy


We are experienced policy advocates and organizers on behalf of our communities facing institutional race and social injustice. We believe systemic change is possible by participating in the political and legislative process.

Community Outreach


We are dedicated to advancing political representation reflective of our communities, and engaging underrepresented communities in the political process. We have co-hosted and participated in forums and panel events in partnership with community and political leaders.
